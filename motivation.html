    Motivation

    Motivation is a process that drives, guides and sustains goal oriented behaviours.

    What is your goal and how are you going to reach it?

    First things first: Make a plan, success involves planning and preparation.

    Secondly: The plan has to be broken down into sub-goals as these help remove the fear and hesitation that can prevent us from achieving life goals.

    Preparation: Is the key to achieving your aims; think and plan for any obstacles that you may face and how you will overcome them.

    Write down: List the benefits of achieving your goals.

    Visual: Donâ€™t hide the list away; place it where you can see it each day as this will be a reminder to you and help reinforce your motivation.

    Tell people: Your friends, family and colleagues about your goals - if your motivation flags they may encourage you on to get back on track.

    Feel good factor: Reward yourself when you achieve a goal - it gives you something to look forward to and reinforces your success.
