Mental Wellbeing @ RGU School of Engineering and School of Computing

Mental wellbeing is critical to excellence in STEM subjects including Engineering and Computing.​

Have you tried turning yourself off and on again? When your computer is slow and unresponsive, you let it rest - why not treat yourself the same way?

This resource has been developed by engineering & computing staff and students to support you through your studies and beyond.​ We chose the name ECHO to represent Engineering and Computing Help Online, but it is a resource open to anyone who is looking for support.
